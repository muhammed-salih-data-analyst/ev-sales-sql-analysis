# Electric Vehicle Sales Analysis | PostgreSQL

End-to-end SQL project analysing a decade of global EV sales data (2015–2024) using PostgreSQL, structured across 5 parts for systematic exploration of market trends, safety insights, and sales-influencing factors.

---

## Project Structure

| Part | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Part 1 | Market & Sales Analysis — top manufacturers, market share, sales growth |
| Part 2 | Changes Over Time — price trends, units sold annually, autonomous level |
| Part 3 | Autonomous Driving & Safety Insights — moving average, safety ratings, top models |
| Part 4 | Factors Influencing Sales — price-sales correlation, warranty vs sales |
| Part 5 | Preference Insights — price segmentation, model percentage share |

---

## Key Findings

- Ferrari led total units sold (2015–2024) with 767,907 units
- Citroen held the highest market share in 2024 at 4.36%
- Total units sold peaked in 2020 at 3,177,723
- Average autonomous level grew from 2.07 (2015) to 2.38 (2021)
- High price segment consistently dominated vehicle counts
- Manufacturers with higher warranty years maintained strong sales volumes
